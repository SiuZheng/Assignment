About

Langkawi: A Tropical Paradise. Discover the enchanting allure of Langkawi, Malaysia's jewel in the Andaman Sea. This archipelago boasts pristine beaches, lush rainforests, and a laid-back atmosphere. Indulge in water sports, explore hidden lagoons, or simply unwind in luxury resorts. Langkawi offers a perfect escape for nature lovers and adventure seekers alike.

Places to Go

  • Dataran Lang

    12M tall sculpture of an eagle

  • Langkawi Cable Car

    Aerial views of the rainforests, islets

  • Ulu Night Market

    45-minute round trip cruise

Travel Tips
