About

Melaka: A Walk Through History. Step back in time in Melaka, a charming city steeped in history and culture. Discover the remnants of Portuguese and Dutch colonial influence in its well-preserved architecture. Explore the vibrant Chinatown and Little India, immersing yourself in the local way of life. Indulge in the rich flavors of Peranakan cuisine, a unique blend of Malay, Chinese, and Indian influences. Melaka offers a captivating journey through the past.

Places to Go

    A former Portuguese Fortress

  • Shore Sky Tower

    Breathtaking view up to 50 km

  • Melaka River Cruise
