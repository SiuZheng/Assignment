Visit the Petronas Twin Towers

Explore the hill’s colonial bungalows, nature trails, and the charming David Brown’s Restaurant.

Explore Jonker Street

Visit Jonker Street, the vibrant heart of Melaka’s Chinatown. Experience the bustling night market, explore historical sites.

Bungee Jumping

Take the plunge at Sunway Lagoon's bungee jumping attraction.
